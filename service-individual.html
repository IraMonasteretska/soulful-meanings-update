What is RTT?

Rapid Transformational Therapy (RTT), created by Marisa Peer, is a powerful therapeutic method that blends the most effective elements of hypnotherapy, CBT, psychotherapy, and NLP.

By accessing the subconscious mind — where long-held beliefs and emotional imprints reside — RTT uncovers the root cause of a challenge and reframes it at its source.
This creates deep, lasting change often far more quickly than traditional talk-based methods.

Who is it For?

What RTT
Can Help With?

Rapid Transformational Therapy is particularly effective for challenges rooted in long-standing beliefs or emotional patterns held in the subconscious mind.
It offers clarity, relief, and deep inner realignment for individuals who experience:

  • low confidence or diminished self-worth
  • anxiety, overwhelm, or persistent self-doubt
  • fears, phobias, or emotional triggers
  • repeating patterns in relationships
  • blocks around success, visibility, or personal identity
  • childhood imprints shaping adult choices
  • feelings of unworthiness, invisibility, or “not enough”
  • habits or behaviours that feel difficult to change

RTT doesn’t simply ease symptoms —
it transforms the belief beneath them.

how a session works

An RTT session guides you into a relaxed, focused state where the subconscious mind becomes accessible.
From this calm inner space, we explore the origins of the issue with clarity and compassion.

A session typically includes:

  • a gentle induction into therapeutic hypnosis
  • exploration of memories connected to the challenge
  • insight into how old beliefs and patterns formed
  • precise reframing to dissolve limiting narratives
  • installation of new, empowering subconscious pathways
  • a personalised transformational audio recording for daily reinforcement

Most clients notice meaningful shifts after one or two sessions —
sometimes immediately, sometimes steadily over the following days
and weeks.

What You May Experience

RTT often creates a noticeable inner shift — a sense that something has
finally loosened, lifted, or aligned.

Clients
frequently describe:

  • emotional lightness and clarity
  • freedom from old patterns or reactions
  • greater confidence and calm

The transformation may unfold instantly, gradually,
